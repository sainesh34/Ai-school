About AI School

AI School is an innovative platform founded by Sai Neshwanth to help degree students — especially those who are sick or handicapped — learn anytime, anywhere.

It includes AI-powered learning, syllabus-wise videos, PDFs, progress tracking, live sessions, and more for BBA, B.Com, B.Sc, and BA students of Telangana & Osmania Universities.
